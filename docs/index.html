Paradise of Printing and Packaging

At SG EDGE we aspire to provide high quality Flexographic, Narrow web flexo, Letter press, Waterless offset roll to roll, finishing/converting and consumables products to labels printing and packaging industries. Working with the renowned global manufacturers from US, Italy, UK, China, Taiwan, Middle East, Africa and India, we endeavour to provide total satisfaction by delivering more of what customers' want - fast response, long list of products, services and strong fleet of experts in the industry. 20 plus years of experience in printing and packaging industry has allowed us to offer remarkable range of products.
